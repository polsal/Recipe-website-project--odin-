Vegan's Rice Plate

Stuff to make with

  • 1 cup / 200g White basmati rice
  • 2 Tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 to 2 Bay leaves ( THANK YOU BORIS )
  • 1/4 to 1/2 Teaspoon Cumin Seeds (I have added 1/2 Teaspoon of cumin)
  • 100g Onion
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Garlic
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Ginger
  • 70g Carrots
  • 70g cup Green Beans - Cut in to 1/2 inch long pieces
  • 70g Frozen Green Peas
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala
  • Salt
  • 350ml Water
  • 50g Toasted cashew nuts

Steps

  1. wash the rice like your mother used to wash you untill the water runs clear
  2. Now let the rice rest and soak in its bath tub for 30 mins
  3. Now unplug the little drainer in the bath tub and watch the fear enter the rice's eyes as it wakes up from its rest . But don't be rude and let it dry of in its strainer
  4. Get a pan add the cumin oil and most importantly ( again thank you boris ) ADD THE BAY LEAF
  5. FRY the mixture but do net let the cumin burn i am watching you garry dont let it burn again
  6. Now chop the onions and add them to the mixture add 1/4 tbsp of salt as well and fry untill onion is soft
  7. Chop the ginger, garlic and add them to the micture frying on medium to medium-low heat for about 1 minute.
  8. Now chop the carrots into cubes and green beans into 1/2 inch long peices . Fry the vegetables on medium heat for about 1 minute.
  9. Then add the rice, garam masala, salt and fry for another 30 seconds to a minute.
  10. Now Add the water and frozen green peas. Bring the water to a vigorous boil.
  11. Once the water starts to boil, cover the pan with a lid and reduce the heat to low.
  12. Cook on low heat until the rice is cooked.
  13. Once the rice is cooked, uncover the pan and cook to remove moisture for a couple of seconds
  14. Turn off the heat.
  15. Add the roasted cashews and mix it VERY GENTLY to prevent the the rice grains from breaking.
  16. DO NOT OVER MIX THE RICE OTHERWISE IT WILL TURN MUSHY.
  17. allow it to rest for 2-3 mins before serving allow it to rest with the lid covered
